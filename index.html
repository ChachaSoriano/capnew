Version 2.1.3

GC CEAS Access Point is a cross platform mobile application that will help the students of College of Education, Arts and Sciences (CEAS) to easily access their grades. It can also help the instructors of CEAS to easily update the grades of their students.

This application is created using HTML5, Javascript and CSS and it is available on BlackBerry World for z10 and q10, Google Play for Android phones and App Store for iOS version.

In order to login, first you need to register an account on our website http://www.gcccs.org/gcceas in the PORTAL section.

You can register an account as STUDENT or FACULTY. Fill-in the required* fields in order to validate your registration. After creating an account you can now access this mobile portal.

You can also view your grades on our website using the same account. Only students who are officially enrolled are allowed to access the portal.

In this application you can view static content of some College information even if you don't have Internet access.
